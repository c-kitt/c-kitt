Formula Racing

Electrical systems work for Brown Formula Racing, focused on wiring schematics and harness build quality.

Project Breakdown

  • Designed detailed wiring schematics and system diagrams in Lucidchart to define electrical architecture and interfaces
  • Fabricated and routed wiring harnesses with controlled labeling, strain relief, and serviceability considerations
  • Assembled electrical subsystems and validated connections through continuity testing and staged power-up checks
  • Integrated sensors, actuators, and control electronics into the vehicle electrical system
  • Improved system reliability through iterative refinement of routing, connectors, and wire management

Technical Stack

  • Lucidchart (Wiring Schematics)
  • Wiring Harness Design
  • Crimping & Connectors
  • Continuity Testing (Multimeter)
  • Power Distribution
  • Vehicle Sensors
  • Actuators
  • DC Electrical Systems
  • Documentation & Revision Control
  • Workshop Electrical Build Practices
